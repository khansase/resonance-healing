Instructions

Resonance Healing.ai enables vibrational remedy information to be entered either by typing in the name of the remedy you want or by typing in a Radionic Rate. Due to the way the app operates, there is no limit as to what a remedy might be. The app serves to synthesise remedy vibrations; remedies are not accessed from an internal database.

Names or rates of remedies required are entered into any one of the three drop-down fields labeled "Set Remedy or Type COPY." More than one remedy can be entered on each line, provided that remedies are separated by either a space or a comma.

Names or rates relating to information entered and then potentised are recorded in the drop-down boxes and may be recalled by clicking on them. Information entered can be cleared temporarily by clicking on the reset button or cleared permanently from the drop-down lists by clicking on the "Clear" button.

When quitting the app, a Notepad file is automatically created in the same directory as the app called "Last Remedy." This file records all information that was entered or changed: the names of remedies, the potencies used, the power level in units, the time period, and even the text and background color you used. This file is updated the next time you make a remedy. If you wish, you can save the information by renaming the "Last Remedy" file (don’t worry, the app will create a new one) or by clicking on File > Save As to save a copy from within the app. To recall saved information, click on File > Open and browse for the saved file location.

The app includes drop-down potency boxes, allowing the creation of homoeopathic remedies from 1 - 9999 X, C, M, CM, MM, and LM or D. If no potency is required, both potency boxes may be cleared. An additional useful feature is a power control calibrated in units. The default setting is 30 units, the power level of standard homoeopathic remedies purchased at a pharmacy. Remedies may be made with power levels of 10 - 1600 units, though higher levels (above 250 units) are for experimental applications only. The app includes timer-controlled energisation. The timer can be set using a drop-down box to provide a time period of 3 - 30 seconds, although adequate potentisation may be achieved with the default 3 seconds.

Two other energy programs are available in the app:

  • Lock Function: Prevents remedies from losing potency if stored near strong-smelling substances or subjected to physical impact.
  • Program Function: Allows you to define specific qualities for your remedy, such as "This remedy will stay in the body for 10 minutes only" or "This remedy will radiate a distance of 2 feet from the bottle."

All buttons in the app are operated by clicking on them, and the "Potentise" button can also be operated by pressing the Enter key.

You can also create a multiple remedy with different potencies for each ingredient. To do so, click on Remedy > Programme, where you can type in as many remedy names and potencies as required.

The app includes a Color Remedy Generator. To access it, click on Remedy > Color Rem. In the rectangle in the middle of the screen, a color picker allows you to select any of thousands of possible colors. You can assign a homoeopathic potency to your color remedy or leave the potency fields blank if it is intended as an energy remedy. The "Last Remedy" file is updated with this information, or you can save it manually by clicking on File > Save As.

The app also includes an Energy Memory feature. This can be activated to store remedies created in the app, allowing them to be broadcast continually even after the app is closed. The Energy Memory also has an erase function to clear stored information when needed.

Procedure for Making a Remedy Vibration:
  1. Open the Radionic Potentiser App by clicking on its icon.
  2. In the field labeled "Enter Remedy Information or Type COPY," type the name of the remedy vibration you want or its rate (e.g., Homoeopathic Remedy, Flower Remedy, Gemstone Remedy). For multiple remedies, separate names with a space or comma.
  3. If a homoeopathic potency is required, select it using the up/down boxes or type in the desired figure. If no potency is required, clear both potency boxes.
  4. Adjust the power level if needed (range: 10 - 1600 units). The default is 30 units.
  5. Click on the "Potentise" button or press Enter. The word "Energising" will appear on the screen, accompanied by the progression of a status bar. A double beep indicates the end of potentisation.
  6. To program the remedy with specific qualities, click on Remedy > Programme, type in your energy program, adjust the potency fields if needed, and click "Potentise."
  7. To lock the remedy vibration, click the "Lock" button and then "Potentise."
  8. Your remedy is now ready for use.

To exit the app, click on File > Exit or the close button.

Procedure for Copying a Remedy:
  1. Place the word “COPY” in the field labeled "Enter Remedy Information or Type COPY."
  2. If a homoeopathic potency is required, select it using the up/down boxes or type in the desired figure. If no potency is required, clear both potency boxes.
  3. Click the "Potentise" button or press Enter to copy the remedy vibration.
Procedure for Broadcasting a Remedy Vibration:
  1. Enter the remedy information and potency as usual.
  2. Activate the Energy Memory feature and store the remedy vibration.
  3. The remedy is now ready for continuous broadcasting.
Procedure for Erasing the Contents of the Energy Memory:
  1. Use the Erase Memory function in the app.
  2. Confirm the erasure when prompted. The memory will be cleared.
